About Jejueo (제주어)

Jejueo, also called Jeju language, is the traditional language of Jeju Island in South Korea. It differs significantly from standard Korean and is considered by many linguists to be a separate language.

With fewer than 10,000 fluent speakers remaining, mostly elderly, Jejueo is classified as critically endangered by UNESCO. Our translator helps preserve this important cultural heritage.
